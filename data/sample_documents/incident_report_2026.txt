INCIDENT REPORT
Report ID: INC-2026-0034
Date occurred: 2026-05-18
Equipment tag: C-220
Title: Compressor vibration event with thermal spike

Description:
During a scheduled compressor run in Unit 3, monitoring personnel detected
an unusual vibration signature in the compressor housing, followed almost
immediately by a temperature spike recorded on the adjacent instrumentation
loop. The event lasted under two minutes and the compressor was
automatically taken offline as a precaution. No equivalent event had been
found in prior compressor maintenance records at the time of filing.

Symptoms observed: unusual vibration, temperature spike, brief automatic
shutdown

Investigation status: Open. No linkage yet established to prior incidents
on other equipment in the same process unit.
