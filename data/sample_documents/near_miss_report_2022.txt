NEAR-MISS REPORT
Report ID: NM-2022-0091
Date occurred: 2022-03-11
Equipment tag: V-204
Title: Gas pressure anomaly during feed valve operation

Description:
During routine operation, shift personnel observed an unusual vibration in
the feed valve assembly accompanied by a sudden temperature spike in the
adjacent piping section. Gas pressure sensor readings showed a transient
spike of 12% above baseline for approximately 90 seconds before returning
to normal. No injury or shutdown occurred. Investigation found that the
pressure sensor data was not cross-referenced against the active permit-to-
work log at the time of the event.

Symptoms observed: unusual vibration, temperature spike, transient gas
pressure spike

Corrective action taken: Verbal briefing to shift team. No procedure change
was issued at this time.
