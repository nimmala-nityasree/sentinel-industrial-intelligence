# SOP-114: Feed Valve Inspection Procedure (Version 2)

**Document ID:** SOP-114-v2
**Effective date:** 2022-03-01
**Applies to:** Equipment tag V-204 (Feed valve, Unit 3)
**Superseded by:** SOP-114 Version 3 (effective 2024-06-01)

## 1. Purpose
This procedure defines the mandatory inspection cadence for feed valve V-204
in Unit 3, following the 2022 near-miss investigation into gas pressure
anomalies in the coke oven battery area.

## 2. Requirement
Feed valve V-204 shall be inspected for seal integrity, actuator response,
and pressure differential every **180 days**. Inspection must be logged as
a completed work order referencing this procedure.

## 3. Responsible personnel
Certified valve technicians only. Personnel must complete SOP-114 training
before being authorized to sign off on V-204 inspections.

## 4. Escalation
Any pressure differential reading outside ±5% of baseline must be reported
to the shift safety officer immediately and logged as a near-miss if no
corrective action is taken within 24 hours.
