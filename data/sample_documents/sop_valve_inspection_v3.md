# SOP-114: Feed Valve Inspection Procedure (Version 3)

**Document ID:** SOP-114-v3
**Effective date:** 2024-06-01
**Applies to:** Equipment tag V-204 (Feed valve, Unit 3)
**Supersedes:** SOP-114 Version 2 (effective 2022-03-01)

## 1. Purpose
This revision tightens the inspection cadence for feed valve V-204 following
an industry-wide advisory on coke oven battery gas accumulation risk.

## 2. Requirement (revised)
Feed valve V-204 shall be inspected for seal integrity, actuator response,
and pressure differential every **180 days**, with an additional mandatory
gas-detector cross-check introduced in this version. Inspection must be
logged as a completed work order referencing this procedure.

## 3. Responsible personnel
Certified valve technicians only. **Personnel trained only on Version 2
must complete Version 3 refresher training before their next inspection
sign-off — the gas-detector cross-check step did not exist in Version 2.**

## 4. Escalation
Unchanged from Version 2: any pressure differential reading outside ±5% of
baseline must be reported to the shift safety officer immediately.
